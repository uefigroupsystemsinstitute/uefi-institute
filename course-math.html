Unit 1: Pure Mathematics — Algebra & Functions

2. Factorise the following trinomial completely: 3x^2 - 14x - 5

3. Solve the simultaneous linear equations for x and y: 2x + 3y = 11 and x - 2y = -5

4. Solve the linear inequality: -3(x - 2) <= 12

5. Determine the vertical and horizontal asymptotes of the hyperbolic function: f(x) = 3 / (x - 2) + 4

6. Find the inverse function f^-1(x) for the linear function f(x) = 4x - 7

7. Identify the coordinates of the turning point for the quadratic function: y = x^2 - 6x + 5

8. If the graph of y = 2^x is shifted 3 units to the right and 1 unit up, what is the new equation?

8. Solve for general solutions: 2cos(θ) - 1 = 0 for θ in range [0°, 360°]
